THE JOOS PODCAST

Who are we?

We are Mike, Frank, and Jacoby

The [Joos] Clan

We met each other on move In day at college and were happy to discover that we each had a passion for all things game related. For us, video games served as a way to momentarily escape the stressors of reality and dive into worlds where power-ups, trophies, and special abilities are limitless. When it came to fist person shooters and fighting games, we’ve always gone by the tag of [JOOS] and so we decided to create a podcast dedicated to highlighting all things “Joosy” in the gaming world.


There’s a game for everyone and we love to share our thoughts on all of them!

Our Show!

Round 1: Poor Connection

A segment about things we don’t really understand. We pose questions to our social media following regarding games that people are hype about that we just don’t understand… but we want to!

Round 2: Rage Quit

A segment of our show where we each critique something we absolutely hate that a game has done. Just a venting session!

Final Round: Scouting Report

Our trio ends the show on a positive vibe, highlighting a game they are enjoying or looking forward to.
